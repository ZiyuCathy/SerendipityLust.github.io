Legal Due Dilligence Logic

Author: ZYderAloha Date: January 18, 2022  16:37:42 Category: knowledge

（up）Ownership structure
shareholders: equity and debt
their key rights: naming board directors to decide critical corporate matters of company business (to conduct business, we need management team, employee, money, equipment)

  • what kind of business? materials, manufacturing, product sales
  • for materials, self-research [US research center?] or be supplied？[US supplier?], purchase and supply policy
  • for manufacturing (materials to products)
    • design: dependent or independent
  • – for dependent, source (licensing or franchising), partners[US partner?], licensing and royalty contract, franchising contract for business cooperation
  • – for independent ones, type, level (number, compared with competitors), future plan, R&D policy [IP cleanliness, IP state(if it is fully developed/commercialized), IP usage (limited/internal/external?)]
    • produce: how? Pains (equipment, labor, facility, location)? [US factory/tool?], equipment/facility/labor-related agreement
  • product sales: current and potential clients, location [US client?], export control compliance policy
    we also need supervisory board to oversee board directors
  • supervise those policies made by executive board

（down） entities：any subsidiaries, joint venture, affiliates, branches with exporting businesses in US? [BIS entity list]
