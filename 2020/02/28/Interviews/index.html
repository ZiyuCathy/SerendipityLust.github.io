Chapter 1 - General Principles

Author: ZY Date: February 28, 2020  9:37:44 Category: QuantInterview

  1. Build a broad knowledge base
    Landing a quant includes brain teaser, calculus, linear algebra, probablity theory, statistics, derivetive pricing and programming.

  2. Practice interview skills
    Bahavior, technical, and resume-related questions can be anticipated.

  3. Listen carefully
    If any aspect is not clear to you, politely ask for clarification.
    If the sentences are too long, write down key words.
    Grasp the clues interviewers give to you, including the assumptions.

  4. Speak your mind
    Always demonstrate your steps involved since your interviewer could give you some clues.

  5. Make reasonable assumptions
    The keyword here is reasonable. Explain the assumptions to the interviewer to get immediate feedbacks.
